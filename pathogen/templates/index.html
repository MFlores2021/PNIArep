Database of the main pathogens detected, identified

and characterized in the potato crop in Peru

Phytophthora infestans and Ralstonia solanacearum affect numerous crops of economic importance worldwide. In Peru, these pathogens are the main phytosanitary problems associated with potato. On the other hand, viruses affect potato crops, so the results generated in the detection of these viruses will allow to better understand the diversity of viruses.
